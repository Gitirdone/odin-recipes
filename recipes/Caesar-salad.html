Caesar Salad

Rich savory dressing tops this vibrant appetizer. Pairs well with Lasagna! Find our recipe here Lasagna

Are you still with me on this assortment of easy cuisines? Good, cause I have one more for you and it is as easy as opening a bag of lettuce and topping it. Its a simple appetizer that you can make even better with homemade dressing. Just a heads up, it has anchovies in it. If you aren't a fan then honestly just stick to store-bought as it often lacks this key essential ingredient. For the sake of Simplicity we are using store bought dressing. Don't blink, you'll be eating salad in 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • One bag of your favorite salad mix
  • Favorite Caesar salad dressing (if I'm forced to use bottled, I go for Panera brand dressing)
  • Croutons
  • Fresh grated parmesan for topping
  • (Optional) Chicken carved into strips. Really adds some protein.

Steps

  1. Open the bag of salad
  2. Pour in a healthy amount of dressing and croutons
  3. Toss bag vigourosly till salad is evenly coated
  4. Dust generously with parmesan cheese and top with your chicken
