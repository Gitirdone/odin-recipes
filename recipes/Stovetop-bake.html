Stovetope Bake

A rich southern comfort dish that both quick to make and delicious!

This dish is made with easiness in mind. Most of these ingredients are already in you're household (I would hope). Simplicity of this dish reminds me why I miss my mother's cooking. It's not oversaturated with expensive ingredients or difficult task. Put it all together in a baking dish, cover with instant stovetop stuffing mix and bake till bubbly. If that needs to be any simpler I don't think that you should be cooking. Offense intended.

Best part about this recipe is that it can be substituted as you see fit. Don't like chicken? Use beef or pork, the base of the recipe is so interchangeble. Prefer not to use creem of chicken? Use another rich soup base, it's your life! Better yet, don't liek vegetables? You know where I'm going with this. But enough ranting, it would be finished by the time you were done reading this description.

What you'll need

  • 4 Tbsps of butter
  • 1 bag of frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 1/2 pounds of chicken
  • 3 cans of Campbells cream of chicken soup
  • 1 bag of packaged Stovetop stuffin mix
  • half a can of soup with water
  • 2 tsps of Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Mix together frozen veggies, cream of chicken soup and half a can of water into a casserole dish.
    3. Add the Thyme and season with Salt and Pepper to taste.
  3. Add in the chicken (you can definitely use rotisserie chicken) and make sure its incorporated.
  4. Top evenly with the bag of stovetop stuffing mix.
  5. Melt the butter and drizzle overtop the stuffing
  6. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until bubbling.
  7. Let it sit for 5 minutes then serve.
